Nutrition

Keep your food nutrients intact. Most of the people spoil the nutritions of their food by cooking it in a wrong way. Washing vegetables after cutiing can wash off the vitamins, overcooking food can spoil the nutrients, using extra oil can turn any healthy food into unhealthy food.

To keep the food nutrients intact, cooking process plays a vital role. So be careful while cooking, if you really want to eat healthy.
