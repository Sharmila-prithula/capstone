Mango juice

Ingredients:

  1. Green Mango
  2. Salt
  3. Sugar
  4. Green chilli
  5. Black salt
  6. Mustard paste

Procedure:

Take each ingredients according to your taste and blend them into a fine paste. After that add some ice cubes and water according to how much consistency you want. Serve it in a glass and enjoyyy.
